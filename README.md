## 👁️ Protect Your Vision
Most people spend 8–12 hours a day in front of screens. **EyeRest** is a powerful, open-source Chrome extension designed to combat digital eye fatigue (Computer Vision Syndrome) using the scientifically-backed **20-20-20 rule**: Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Sequential Timer Intelligence:** The interval timer pauses during your break and only restarts once you've completed your eye exercises.
- **Extendable Breaks:** Need more rest? Instantly add +10s, +20s, or +30s to your current break with one click.
- **Guided Exercise Suite:** Beautifully animated, ophthalmologist-approved exercises that cycle automatically to relieve eye muscle tension.
- **Adaptive Glassmorphism UI:** A stunning interface that perfectly matches your OS Light or Dark mode preferences.
- **Zero-Impact Performance:** Built on Manifest V3 using `chrome.alarms`, ensuring near-zero CPU and battery usage.
- **100% Privacy-First:** No accounts, no data collection, no analytics. Everything stays on your local machine.

## 🛠️ Installation (Developer Mode)
1. Clone this repository: `git clone https://github.com/drdhavaltrivedi/EyeRest-Chrome-Extension.git`
2. Open Google Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Enable **Developer mode** in the top-right corner.
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the folder where you cloned the repository.
5. Pin **EyeRest** to your toolbar for easy access!
